Writing a research paper as a beginner can be a daunting task, but with a structured approach,
it becomes manageable. Here are some steps to guide you through the process:
1. Choose a Topic
• Interest and Relevance: Select a topic that interests you and is relevant to the current
trends in your field.
• Feasibility: Ensure the topic is feasible in terms of available resources, time, and data.
2. Conduct Preliminary Research
• Literature Review: Read existing papers and articles to understand the background
and identify gaps in the research.
• Formulate Research Questions: Based on your literature review, develop specific
research questions or hypotheses.
3. Plan Your Research
• Methodology: Decide on the methods you will use to collect and analyze data (e.g.,
experiments, surveys, simulations).
• Timeline: Create a timeline with milestones to keep your research on track
